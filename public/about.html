Our Story

WiseCloud Inc. formerly known as Sportality Asia Inc., is a recruitment process outsourcing company that offers professional, trusted, flexible offshore IT support services for Swedish companies

WiseCloud Inc. was founded by Mr. Lasse Wöien, Norwegian and two (2) other Swedish entrepreneurs and was established in the Philippines in 2020.

We believe in Mission To expand the sustainable workforce of our employees and deliver support and IT services for Swedish companies.

2020

WiseCloud Inc started as “Sportality Asia Inc” in 2020 when its mother company “Sportality AB” in Sweden hired 2 software developers in The Philippines through a partner.

2021

A great partnership achieved a common goal. WiseCloud Inc. was officially founded.

2022

Expansions, new challenges & growth for the company.
